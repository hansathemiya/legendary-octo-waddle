DEPLOYMENT PLAN

Marketing Workflow
& Execution Roadmap

A strategic timeline for transforming one accounting tutor into the leading education authority in Sri Lanka.
Optimization • Content • Funnels • Scaling

PHASE 01 // FOUNDATION

Account Optimization

Audit Branding Uniformity

Audit all existing social media accounts. Edit layouts, bios, and usernames to align with the new elite brand identity.

Strategy Logic
This stage ensures the foundation is strong. Every account must reflect the same brand persona, tone, and authority before traffic is driven.
PHASE 02 // FOOTPRINT

Account Expansion

Multi-Channel TikTok LinkedIn YouTube

Create new accounts on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, Facebook, and Twitter/X for aggressive marketing and funnel testing.

Platform Roles
TikTok for reach, YouTube for authority, LinkedIn for credibility, Instagram for engagement, Facebook for parent decision-makers.
PHASE 03 // INTELLIGENCE

Research & Content Mapping

Competitor Analysis Theme Mapping

Conduct full-scale research into audience behavior. Map content themes: “the whys, the whats, the hows” to build a continuous narrative.

Content Flow
Research ensures content is not random. Mapping themes creates a structured narrative that resonates with student pain points and exam cycles.
PHASE 04 // ENGAGEMENT

Micro-Interaction Funnel

Polls Q&A DMs

Deploy polls, Q&A stickers, and DM automations. Capture early leads and test responsiveness to specific topics.

Lead Capture
Micro-interactions build trust. They act as the first low-friction touchpoints to convert passive viewers into active leads.
PHASE 05 // PRODUCTION

Podcast & Clipping Engine

Long-Form Shorts Reels

Launch a podcast or lecture series. Clip episodes into short, engaging snippets optimized for retention across all platforms.

Dual Presence
Long-form builds deep authority and trust. Short-form clips maximize reach and virality. Together, they dominate the feed.
PHASE 06 // RELATABILITY

Meme-Culture Edits

Viral Phonk Edits Gen-Z

Introduce relatable edits. Example: Ronaldo phonk edits with captions like “How I feel after writing 250 words for a 200-word essay.”

Humanization
Meme culture humanizes the tutor, making them approachable. This drastically increases shareability among the student demographic.
PHASE 07 // R&D

Trialing & Templates

A/B Testing Data Analysis Efficiency

Test different hooks and styles. Analyze performance data to identify winners, then build working templates for replication.

Optimization
Templates reduce workload and ensure consistency. Trialing ensures only proven formats are scaled to the main account.
PHASE 08 // CONVERSION

Structured Funnel Creation

Lead Magnet Nurture Close

Design the primary funnel path: Awareness (Social) → Engagement (Lead Magnet) → Conversion (Counseling) → Enrollment.

Predictability
Funnels provide revenue predictability. This stage ensures every social media view has a clear path to becoming a paying student.
PHASE 09 // RHYTHM

Stable Content System

Consistency Retention

Transition into a consistent posting rhythm on main accounts focusing on "How, What, Why, Who". Trial accounts continue testing in background.

Sustainability
Stability ensures long-term retention. Trial accounts act as innovation labs without disrupting the brand integrity of the main channel.
PHASE 10 // DOMINANCE

SEO & Geo-Targeting

Local SEO Google Maps Heatmapping

Deploy geo-targeted SEO strategies to dominate "Accounting Class" searches in Sri Lanka. Build a parallel web funnel for lead listing.

Search Authority
SEO ensures discoverability outside of social algorithms. Geo-targeting locks down the specific Sri Lankan education niche.
PHASE 11 // AUTOMATION

Setup Phase Completion

Self-Sustaining CRM Integration

The system is now self-sustaining. Leads flow in, content maintains visibility, and backend systems handle the pipeline.

Operational Ready
Marketing is now a machine, not a manual task. The team shifts focus to management and optimization.
PHASE 12 // APOCALYPSE

Aggressive Scaling

Paid Ads Max Volume

Shift into aggressive production. Leverage pre-built datasets. Introduce strategic paid ads to amplify proven organic hits.

Exponential Growth
Scaling is the final stage. We use paid budgets only on content that has already proven itself organically to ensure maximum ROI.
