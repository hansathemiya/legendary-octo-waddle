Company Overview

GeoSafe Evaluations is a specialized geological risk assessment service focused on protecting lives and assets in Sri Lanka’s landslide-prone regions. We offer comprehensive, on-site evaluations and actionable recommendations to mitigate geological risks for homeowners, landowners, developers, and public institutions.

The Problem

Sri Lanka’s hilly regions face increasing landslide and soil instability risks due to:

  • Climate variability and intense rainfall triggering slope failures.
  • Lack of accessible, affordable, professional geological evaluations for decision-makers.
  • Most safety decisions made without proper geological data or risk assessments.

Consequences include:

  • Loss of homes and property.
  • Construction delays and failures.
  • Increase in insurance and social costs.
  • Human safety risks.

Our Solution: GeoSafe Evaluations

We deliver:

  • On-site geological risk assessments.
  • Structured risk reports (PDF).
  • Soil and slope analysis.
  • Landslide history research.
  • Practical mitigation recommendations.

Services include:

  • Basic Geo Check
  • Standard Risk Assessment
  • Advanced Geological Report
  • Premium Developer Package
  • Add-ons: Drone mapping, lab testing, drainage analysis, etc.
